# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics**, a comprehensive guide to understanding the intersection of artificial intelligence and humanoid robot systems. This book explores how AI technologies enable robots to interact with the physical world in human-like ways.

## About This Book

This book provides a structured approach to understanding Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics, covering fundamental concepts through to advanced applications. Each chapter builds upon previous knowledge to create a comprehensive understanding of this rapidly evolving field.

## What You'll Learn

- Fundamentals of humanoid design and biomechanics
- Control systems for stable and purposeful movement
- Sensing and perception for environmental awareness
- AI and machine learning techniques for robotic applications
- Current applications and future directions

## Getting Started

Navigate through the chapters using the sidebar to explore topics in a structured manner. Each chapter includes detailed explanations, practical examples, and summaries to reinforce your learning.
